IRON MAN:A SUPER HERO

TONY STARK A genius. A billionaire. An inventor. But one mission changed everything...

One day, while showing his latest invention, Tony was attacked and captured by dangerous enemies. During the attack, pieces of metal became trapped near his heart.

In a dark cave, Tony met a kind scientist named Yinsen. Together, they built a small glowing device called the Arc Reactor to keep Tony alive

But Tony had a bigger plan. Using scraps of metal, wires, and tools, he secretly built a powerful suit of armor. When the suit was ready, he escaped the cave and flew to freedom.

Back home, Tony realized that technology should help people, not harm them. He worked day and night to build a faster, stronger, and smarter suit.

Soon, he could soar through the sky, protect innocent people, and stop powerful villains.

From that day on, Tony Stark was no longer just an inventor. He became... IRON MAN!

A hero powered by courage, intelligence, and a heart that never gave up.

THE END
